#Welcome to Bioparque Quillón

Presentation

Bioparque Quillón is a developing project consisting of a non-profit, secular, apolitical, and non-judgmental institution whose work focuses on animals and their present and future welfare and conservation.

Its inception is due to the initiative of its Director, Mr. Cristián Anguita Geissbühler and his Mrs. Patricia Campos Lara, who, supported by Sociedad Agrícola y Forestal El Retiro Ltda., acquired the land in 2010 where the construction of Bioparque Quillón began, later giving physical existence to the Foundation for the Protection of Wildlife...
