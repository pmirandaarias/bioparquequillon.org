#Welcome to Bioparque Quillón

About Us

Bioparque Quillón is a developing project consisting of a non-profit, secular, apolitical, and unbiased institution, whose primary focus is on animals and their wellbeing, both in the present and for the future.

It was founded through the initiative of its Director, Mr. Cristián Anguita Geissbühler, and his wife Mrs. Patricia Campos Lara, who, supported by Sociedad Agrícola y Forestal El Retiro Ltda., acquired in 2010 the land where the construction of Bioparque Quillón began, which later gave physical existence to the Foundation for the Protection of Wildlife...
